\section{Notation}
\label{sec:notation}

This section defines the notation used in subsequent sections.

Unless stated otherwise, every quantity is an 18-decimal fixed-point
integer (WAD, scale $10^{18}$): NAV amounts, rates, ratios, senior
tranche shares, and BPT amounts alike. The single exception is raw
quote-asset amounts, which keep their token's native decimals (e.g.\
$10^6$ for USDC).
